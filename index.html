About

More About Me.

My name is Kostya, I'm 19. I've been creating and developing websites, web applications, and their support for a year. At the moment I'm a junior but I strive for more and I can realize myself in a good team. About myself, I can say that I am responsible, punctual, and stress resistant.

I became interested in the Front-End developer specialty a year ago and since then I have not stopped studying it for a single day. I decided to start with introductory courses (HTML, CSS, JS) and graduated with honors! After that, I decided to deepen my skills in this profession. I studied JavaScript, jQuery, tried Gulp and SCSS, and also tried React, Node.js, TypeScript. Also dealt with GIT and its system of repositories, branches, request pool, and so on.

For a year I managed to try myself as a freelancer. But after working there for 3 months, I realized that I was not developing there as a specialist and was standing still. I also tried to do a personal project but the project was curtailed due to lack of funds.
